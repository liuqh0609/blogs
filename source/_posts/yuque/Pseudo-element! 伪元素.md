- ::before
- ::after
- ::first-line 选中第一行
- ::first-letter 选中第一个字母

```html
<div>
  <::before/> content content content content content content content content
  <::after/>
</div>
```

```html
<div>
  <::first-letter>c</::first-letter>ontent content content content
  content content content content
</div>
```
